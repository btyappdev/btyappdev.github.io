1. Introduction

UMax ("UMax", "we", "us", or "our") is an iOS app that gives you AI-generated facial analysis, appearance scores, a personalized glow-up coaching plan, and related "fun" features such as celebrity matching, color analysis, and AI try-on image generation.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use UMax, how we use and share it, how long we keep it, and the choices and rights you have — including how to delete your photos and account. It applies to everyone who uses the app, including users who sign in anonymously as a guest.

If you have questions about this policy or your data, contact us at betultosunios@gmail.com.

2. Information We Collect

a. Photos and Face Data

This is the most important category of data UMax handles. When you use the app, you take three selfies (front, side, and smile) for AI facial analysis, and you may upload additional photos for Fun Features (Celebrity Match, Color Analysis, Ideal Version) or Try-On (hairstyle, beard, hair color, skin color generation). We refer to these collectively as your photos and face data.

b. Account Information

By default, UMax signs you in anonymously so you can use the app as a guest without providing any personal details. If you choose to link an account, we collect the identifiers needed for Sign in with Apple, Google Sign-In, or email/password, including your email address if you provide one. Your email, when provided, is used for account access and may be set as a user attribute in our analytics and subscription tools.

c. Purchase Information

Subscriptions and credit packs are purchased through Apple's In-App Purchase system. We never see or store your card number or other payment card details — Apple handles payment processing. We and our subscription management provider receive purchase and entitlement information (such as which product you bought and its status) needed to unlock features.

d. Usage and Device Data

We collect analytics events such as app usage, scan started/completed with score, results viewed or shared, paywall interactions, feature usage, invite activity, and error/diagnostic data. These events are tied to an internal user ID, not to your photos.

e. Content You Submit

If you contact us with feedback or support requests, we collect what you send us. If you use the invite/referral system, we collect the invite codes you send or redeem and related credit activity.

3. Face Data and AI Processing

Because your photos are sensitive, we want to be specific about why we collect them, how long we keep them, and who they are shared with.

Why we collect your face photos

We collect your selfies and uploaded photos solely to generate the analysis, scores, recommendations, and images you request — for example, your attractiveness and per-feature scores, your coaching plan, celebrity matches, color analysis, an idealized version of your face, or a try-on image of a new hairstyle or color.

How we process your photos

When you take or upload a photo, the app first checks on-device that a face is present. The photo is then JPEG-compressed and securely transmitted to our backend, built on Google Cloud and Firebase infrastructure. Our backend then engages third-party AI service providers, acting as our processors, to produce your results:

  • OpenAI — performs the facial analysis that produces your scores, tier, and recommendations.
  • Eachlabs — performs the AI image generation used for Try-On, Ideal Version, and related visual features.

These providers process your photos transiently, solely to generate the result you requested. They act under processor obligations: they are not permitted to retain your photos beyond what is needed to produce your result, to use them to train their models, or to use them for any purpose of their own. Your photos are not added to any third-party training dataset.

Your explicit consent is required first. Before the first time you use any camera or photo feature, the app asks for your explicit consent to this AI processing. You can decline, in which case no photo is captured, uploaded, or processed. You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account or contacting us.

How long we keep them

Your photos, the scores generated from them, and any generated images are stored on our servers (Google Firebase Storage) for as long as your account is active, because our scan history, progress tracking, and try-on/fun-feature history features depend on this data. They are deleted when you delete your account. See Data Retention for details.

Who we share them with

Your photos and face data are shared only with the cloud infrastructure and third-party AI processors we use to produce your results. We never sell your photos or face data, and we do not share them for advertising purposes.

We do not use your photos to identify you. UMax does not build facial-recognition templates, faceprints, or any other biometric identifier for the purpose of identifying you or anyone else. Your photos are used only to produce the scores, recommendations, and images described in this app.

  • Maintain, secure, and improve the app, including diagnosing errors
  • Communicate with you about your account or in response to your requests

Where the GDPR or similar laws apply, we rely on the following legal bases:

  • Performance of a contract — to provide the core features you request, such as analysis, scores, coaching, and try-on results.
  • Legitimate interests — to secure the app, prevent abuse of the referral system, and improve our features through aggregated analytics.
  • Consent — for optional features such as the daily reminder notification and, where required by local law, certain analytics or processing of your photos.
